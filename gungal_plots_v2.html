Documents Guide

What We Always
Verify

Before we list or sell any plot, we check these documents. Buyers can be confident.

🌿
Dharani Portaldharani.telangana.gov.in — Ownership & land records
🗺️
Meebhoomimeebhoomi.telangana.gov.in — Adangal records
📜
TS-RERA Portalrera.telangana.gov.in — Project registrations

✅ Must-Check Documents

  • 1
    Pattadar PassbookMain ownership proof — must match seller's name on Dharani.
  • 2
    Adangal (Village Land Record)Shows land type, area, and who is using the land currently.
  • 3
    Encumbrance Certificate (EC)Confirms no bank loan or court case pending on the land.
  • 4
    Sale Deed / Title DeedPrevious purchase document — shows the chain of ownership.
  • 5
    Survey Number (Dharani)Verified on Dharani portal before every listing.

📋 Good to Have

  • A
    DTCP / HMDA Layout ApprovalNeeded for bank loans. Confirms government approval.
  • B
    Gram Panchayat NOCFor village plots — confirms no local government objection.
  • C
    Land Conversion CertificateAgricultural land converted to residential — must verify.
⚠️ Always consult a lawyer before finalising any land deal. We guide you, but legal decisions need expert advice.
